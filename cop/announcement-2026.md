# Tipsport Conquest of Prague 2026
- Start Date: 2026-04-12
- End Date: 2026-05-24
- Location: Online / LAN Prague
- Teams: 16
- Announcement + additional information: https://www.playzone.cz/clanky/228536-tipsport-conquest-prague-se-vraci-se-svym-druhym-rocnikem-co-na-hrace-ceka
---
## Tournament Format
- 16-team tournament consisting of 12 invited teams and 4 qualified teams with online/LAN stages
- From April 12 to 27, all 16 teams will play 5-round online Swiss Stage, all matches are broadcasted
- 0-0, 0-1, 1-0 and 1-1 matches of Swiss Stage are BO1, all remaining matches are played as BO3
- 3-0 teams advance directly to the LAN Finals, 3-1 and 3-2 teams moves into the online Decider Stage
- Decider Stage consists of a 6-team Single Elimination bracket, two teams qualify to the LAN Finals
- From May 22 to 24, the top 4 teams from all online stages will compete in a live tournament in Prague
## Additional Information
- A definition of the filters to the VRS that Direct VRS Invites will be based on: March VRS Europe ranking excluding teams that include players from Russia, and Kazakhstan
- The VRS rank from which tournament organizer will begin invitations: Rank 13
- The number of rosters subject to a Direct VRS Invite: Total of 12 invited teams
- Direct invitations will be sent immediately after the publication of the VRS Europe in March
- The VRS publication date from which Direct VRS Invites will be issued: March VRS Europe ranking
- The VRS publication date which will be used for seeding: March VRS Europe ranking
- The visa region where the tournament takes place: Schengen
- Whether an Open Qualifier will be part of the tournament: Total of 4 open qualifiers / 1x CZ/SK, 3x EU (teams with players from Russia, Belarus, and Kazakhstan cannot participate)
- Which tournament stages are online or LAN: Swiss + decider stage are online: Playoff LAN
- The prize pool and the rules for its distribution: 30 000 € (20 000 € / 6 500 € / 2 500 € / 1 000 €)
- Teams are reimbursed for all travel and accommodation costs incurred during the LAN
- Any integrity or conduct rules that disqualify a Roster from a Direct VRS Invite: Teams with players from Russia, and Kazakhstan cannot participate in the tournament
