# Tipsport Open Cup #1
- Start Date: 2026-08-05
- End Date: 2026-08-09
- Region: Worldwide (Buy-in LAN)
- Location: Prague, Czechia
- Venue: Vodafone PLAYzone Arena
- Announcement + additional information: https://www.playzone.cz/clanky/229447-dnes-vecer-spustime-registrace-na-nas-mezinarodni-turnaj-tipsport-open-cup-1
---
## Registration Process
- Registration opens: 5 June 2026, 8 PM (CEST)
- Registration ends: 22 July 2026, 8 PM (CEST)
- Entry fee: €1,000 + VAT
- Fill the official team application form: https://forms.office.com/e/kG0u9RwB6S
- Details with the payament terms will be sent to the contact used during registration
- You have 48 hours to complete the payment and provide proof of payment
- Once we receive the payment along with confirmation that it has been made, your registration will become valid
- If you do not complete the payment within the given timeframe, your spot will be offered to the next team on the waiting list
- A total of 12 teams will be accepted in order of payment proof received
- Full list of registered teams: will be added once registration opens
## Tournament Format
- Group Stage: 5–6 August (Round Robin)
- Playoffs: 7–9 August (Double Elimination)
- All matches are Best-of-Three
- Seeding based on the July VRS ranking
- Top four seeded teams advance directly to Playoffs
- For more details, check out the landing page: https://www.playzone.cz/tipsport-open-cup-1
