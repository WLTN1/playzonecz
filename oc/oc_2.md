# Tipsport Open Cup #2

- Start Date: 2026-09-23

- End Date: 2026-09-27

- Region: Worldwide (Buy-in LAN)

- Location: Prague, Czechia

- Venue: Vodafone PLAYzone Arena

- Announcement + additional information: https://www.playzone.cz/clanky/229705-dnes-vecer-spoustime-registrace-na-nas-mezinarodni-turnaj-tipsport-open-cup-2

---

## Registration Process

- Registration opens: 23 July 2026, 8 PM (CEST)

- Registration ends: 9 September 2026, 8 PM (CEST)

- Entry fee: €1,000 + VAT

- Fill the official team application form: https://forms.office.com/e/cM0tjANhzm

- Details with the payment terms will be sent to the contact used during registration

- You have 48 hours to complete the payment and provide proof of payment

- Once we receive the payment along with confirmation that it has been made, your registration will become valid

- If you do not complete the payment within the given timeframe, your spot will be offered to the next team on the waiting list

- A total of 12 teams will be accepted in order of payment proof received

- Full list of registered teams: will be added once registration opens

## Tournament Format

- Group Stage: 23–24 September (Round Robin)

- Playoffs: 25–27 September (Double Elimination)

- All matches are Best-of-Three

- Seeding based on the September VRS ranking

- Top four seeded teams advance directly to Playoffs

- For more details, check out the landing page: https://www.playzone.cz/tipsport-open-cup-2
